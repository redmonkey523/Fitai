# Meal Planning - Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

### Accessing Meal Planning
1. Open the app
2. Navigate to the **Nutrition** tab (bottom navigation)
3. Tap the **calendar icon** in the top right corner
4. You're now in the Meal Planning screen!

## Core Features

### 1. Creating Your First Meal Plan

#### Option A: Add Individual Meals
1. Find the day you want to plan
2. Tap "Add Breakfast" (or Lunch, Dinner, Snack)
3. Browse or search for a recipe
4. Tap the recipe to add it to your meal plan
5. View updated nutrition totals for the day

#### Option B: Generate a Weekly Plan
1. Tap the "Generate Weekly Plan" button at the top
2. Confirm the generation
3. The app will automatically fill in recipes for the entire week
4. Modify any meals you want to change

### 2. Managing Recipes

#### Creating a Recipe
1. From Meal Planning, tap the book icon (top right) ‚Üí Recipe Browser
2. Tap the "+" button in the top right
3. Add a photo (optional but recommended)
4. Fill in basic information:
   - Recipe name *
   - Description
   - Prep time *
   - Cook time *
   - Servings *
   - Difficulty level
5. Select category (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack)
6. Add dietary tags (Vegan, Keto, etc.)
7. Add ingredients:
   - Tap "+" to add more ingredients
   - Enter name, quantity, and unit
   - Tap "X" to remove an ingredient
8. Add instructions:
   - Write step-by-step instructions
   - Tap "+" to add more steps
9. Add nutrition info (optional but helpful)
10. Add notes (optional)
11. Tap "Save"

#### Finding Recipes
1. Go to Recipe Browser
2. Use the search bar to find specific recipes
3. Filter by category (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)
4. Apply dietary filters for your needs
5. Tap any recipe to view details
6. Long-press on a meal in your plan to remove it

#### Favoriting Recipes
1. Open any recipe
2. Tap the heart icon (top right)
3. Access all favorites from Recipe Browser ‚Üí Favorites tab

### 3. Using the Grocery List

#### Auto-Generate from Meal Plans
1. Plan your meals for the week first
2. Navigate to Grocery List (cart icon from Meal Planning)
3. Tap "Generate"
4. Confirm the generation
5. All ingredients from your meal plans are now in your list!

#### Manual Item Addition
1. Open Grocery List
2. Tap "Add Item"
3. Enter item name, quantity, unit, and category
4. Tap "Add"

#### Shopping Mode
1. As you shop, tap the circle next to each item to check it off
2. Checked items appear dimmed
3. After shopping, tap the trash icon ‚Üí "Clear Checked Items"
4. Or start fresh with "Clear All Items"

## Pro Tips

### Meal Planning
- **Today is highlighted** in blue for easy reference
- **Nutrition totals** show at the top of each day
- **Week navigation** buttons let you plan ahead or review past weeks
- **Long press** on any meal to quickly remove it

### Recipe Management
- **Adjust servings** in recipe details to auto-scale ingredients and nutrition
- **Add to Grocery List** button on recipe details adds all ingredients at once
- **Edit recipes** anytime by opening them and tapping the edit icon (if you created them)
- **Search is powerful** - it searches recipe names, descriptions, and ingredients

### Grocery Lists
- **Items auto-categorize** (Produce, Meat, Dairy, etc.) for organized shopping
- **Duplicate items combine** - adding "2 cups Milk" when "1 cup Milk" exists will merge to "3 cups Milk"
- **Generate multiple times** - generating from meal plans adds to existing items, doesn't replace

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Recipes not showing in browser
- **Solution**: Create your first recipe, or check filters aren't excluding all results

### Issue: Generated meal plan is empty
- **Solution**: You need at least a few recipes in each category first

### Issue: Nutrition totals seem wrong
- **Solution**: Check that nutrition info was entered correctly in each recipe

### Issue: Can't edit a recipe
- **Solution**: You can only edit recipes you created. Public recipes are read-only.

### Issue: Grocery list has duplicates
- **Solution**: Items are considered duplicates only if name AND unit match exactly

## Data Privacy
